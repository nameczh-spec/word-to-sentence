## 一、英语一大作文（图画作文）

### 2024年 - 城市公园建设（公园数量与市民满意度）

**题目：** 左图漫画：一个人感叹家门口的市民公园越来越方便，绿地公园有人长跑。右图数据表：2020-2022某市公园数量从460+增加到670+。

**参考范文：**

As is vividly depicted in the picture, a resident is exclaiming with delight that the public park near his home is becoming increasingly convenient. Meanwhile, a bar chart on the right illustrates the dramatic increase in the number of parks in a certain city from over 460 in 2020 to more than 670 in 2022.

The implication of this combination is clear: urban green spaces are expanding significantly, bringing tangible benefits to citizens' quality of life. Several factors contribute to this positive trend. Firstly, the government has placed greater emphasis on ecological civilization construction and invested heavily in public infrastructure. Secondly, as living standards improve, people's demand for recreational and fitness facilities has grown substantially.

From my perspective, the increase in public parks reflects the harmonious coexistence between urban development and environmental protection. It is a commendable achievement that should be sustained and expanded. Only by prioritizing green development can we create more livable and sustainable cities for future generations.

### 2023年 - 传统文化传承

**题目：** 漫画中，儿子问父亲画上的文字是什么，父亲答不上来。反映传统文化流失问题。

**参考范文：**

What the picture above illustrates in a straightforward way is the current situation of our traditional Chinese culture. Ironically, the conversation between the boy and his father deserves our due attention. When asked what these characters are on the painting, the father could not come up with a certain answer, which is ridiculous but thought-provoking.

It is mostly likely that by portraying this drawing the author intends to set us thinking more about the losing of traditional culture. Obviously, there are several reasons accounting for the culture recession. To begin with, modern society is filled with all kinds of advanced technologies and vast quantities of information, so that few people will pay attention to those ancient Chinese characters. In addition, people's awareness of popularizing our national culture is not so strong, which is decisive to the worse condition.

From my perspective, it is imperative for our nation to realize the fact that Chinese culture is the most esteemed culture in the world and should be inherited from generation to generation. For one thing, some effective and feasible measures should be taken to advocate and carry forward the fine culture. For another thing, the effort from our education system is one of the channels to develop the proud Chinese culture traditions. Only by the joint effort, can we construct the harmonious socialism society and create civilizations.

### 2022年 - 言传身教（榜样的力量）

**题目：** 两幅对比漫画。左图：父亲在沙发上踢球，儿子皱眉头做作业。右图：父亲在旁边工作，儿子在旁边认真学习。

**参考范文：**

What is symbolically depicted in the caricatures, there emerge two circumstances, carrying sharp contrast implications. In the first drawing, a father is watching a football match on the sofa who is at the same time supervising his son to finish homework. It is not difficult to observe that his son wears frowned expression on his face. On the contrary, the second portrayal depicts a father working just beside his son, who is doing his assignment.

The objective of the drawer is to demonstrate that utmost significance should be attached to the phenomenon that setting proper examples has exerted great impact on the growth of the younger generation in China at present. Previously, it is widely acknowledged that it is the compelling obligation for the parents to help their kids to form an appropriate value about the world and the life, which carries overwhelmingly precious connotation to the sound development on the younger generation.

In my view, parents should be aware that their behavior speaks louder than words. Children learn more from what they observe than from what they are told. Therefore, it is essential for parents to set good examples in daily life, as this will profoundly shape their children's character and future development.

### 2020年 - 良好的时间管理习惯

**题目：** 两幅图对比。左图：女孩在做作业，说"早完成比较好"。右图：男孩坐在书桌前，说"要最后一分钟才完成"。

**参考范文：**

Portrayed distinctively by the two cartoons above is an impressive scene: a girl in the left picture is doing homework and saying that early completion is better. Nevertheless, the boy in the right picture is sitting in front of the desk and saying that he will not finish the homework until the last minute.

Undoubtedly, the symbolic implication of the pictures is to show us that importance should be attached to the formation of good habits, especially the good habit of time management. On the one hand, efficient time management is critical to personal development. As the old saying goes, "Time is money," and in the fast-paced modern life, we have to realize that efficiency holds the key to saving time and time management skills hold the key to personal success. On the other hand, good time management habits play a vital role in the development of the whole society. Social progress is closely related to the efforts of each individual.

From what has been mentioned above, we can come to the conclusion that the sense of efficient time management skills is of equal importance in personal and social progress. Therefore, we ought to take advantage of the phenomenon to enlighten the public and the press is expected to take a lead in advertising the value of developing good time management habits. Only in this way can we have a bright future.

### 2019年 - 坚持（途中）

**题目：** 两个人在爬山，一个人累了想放弃，另一个人递水鼓励他继续前行。

**参考范文：**

The cartoon provides us with a thought-provoking scene: two men are climbing a hill, but one of them desires to give up due to tiredness, while the other one encourages him to keep going.

Undoubtedly, the cartoonist aims at reminding us of the significance of persistence. At the top of the list, we should attach importance to perseverance mainly due to that it can enable us to ameliorate ourselves so we can be qualified for future career promotion, and be ready for meeting the forthcoming challenges. What's more, we ought to place a high value on the role played by persistence in personal growth. Put it another way, in this ever-changing world, perseverance is to personal growth what water is to fish. To sum up, if persistence misses our attention in any possible way, we will suffer a great loss beyond imagination.

Hence, it is vital for us to derive positive implications from the above cartoon. For one thing, we should frequently use it to enlighten the young. For another, we should cultivate the awareness of teenagers that it is very vital to keep going toward our goals. Only by doing so, can we become winner in the face of difficulties.

### 2018年 - 选课进行时

**题目：** 一个学生坐在电脑前选课，考虑创新、知识新鲜度、难度、得分、通过率、作业量等多种因素。

As is vividly depicted in the graph that sitting in front of a computer, a student is selecting the curriculum, taking numerous elements into consideration including the innovation, the freshness of knowledge and the level of difficulty as well as the scoring, the qualified rate and burden of assignment. We can deduce from the portrayal that well-rounded contemplating, a significant quality, serves as an indispensable capability bringing about positive influence toward different aspects of our lives. It is commonly known that the value of comprehensiveness and sophistication of thinking has been proved by countless examples around us, such as the governmental policy making and selection of compulsory and optional course. If we can put this notion into practice, it would exert profound and constructive influence. Therefore, well-rounded and thorough thinking will create huge benefit to the prosperity of community as well as personal development.

Whatever difficulty or situation we are confronted with, those who have the ability of analyzing the ingredients and integrating them together are nearer to success. Therefore, well-rounded consideration is the wisdom we should live up to.

### 2016年 - 父母的榜样作用

**题目：** 两幅漫画对比。左图：父亲玩电脑，呵斥孩子学习。右图：父亲陪孩子一起学习。

**参考范文：**

As is vividly shown in the two drawings, there exist two contrasting situations concerning the relationship between parents and children. In the first picture, a father is sitting comfortably in a sofa, watching TV and playing games, while at the same time urging his son to study. As a result, the boy seems reluctant and unhappy. In the second picture, however, the father is working diligently at his desk, and the son, following his father's example, is concentrating on his homework.

The purpose of the drawer is to demonstrate that parents' behavior and example play a vital role in the growth of their children. It is generally acknowledged that children are highly influenced by their parents, as they spend most of their time with them. A good example set by parents can encourage children to develop good habits and values, while a bad one may lead to negative consequences.

From my perspective, parents should be aware of their crucial role in their children's education. They should not only tell their children what is right but also set good examples through their own actions. As the saying goes, "Example is better than precept." Only by doing so can children grow up healthily both physically and mentally.

### 2004年 - 终点又是新起点

**题目：** 一个人跑完一圈到终点，但终点又是个新起点，体现坚持奋斗、不断进取的精神。

**参考范文：**

As is vividly shown in the picture, a runner is reaching the finishing line, sweating all over, but he has to continue his running immediately. The caption reads: "The finish line is also a new starting point."

What the picture aims to convey is that we should never stop making progress. In other words, we should always be prepared for new challenges and keep moving forward. This is particularly true in today's rapidly changing world, where knowledge is being updated constantly and competition is becoming increasingly fierce. If we are satisfied with what we have achieved and stop improving ourselves, we will soon lag behind.

Therefore, it is essential for us to cultivate the habit of continuous learning and self-improvement. Only by doing so can we keep pace with the times and achieve greater success in our life and career.

## 二、英语二大作文（图表作文）

### 2024年 - 城市公园数量增长

**题目：** 图表显示2020-2022年某市公园数量从460多个增加到670多个，反映城市绿化建设成果。

**参考范文：**

As is clearly presented in the chart, the number of parks in a certain city has experienced a steady increase from about 460 in 2020 to over 670 in 2022, representing a remarkable growth of more than 45% within just three years.

There are several factors contributing to this phenomenon. First and foremost, with the rapid development of economy, the government has been investing more in public infrastructure and environmental protection. Secondly, as people's living standards improve, there is a growing demand for recreational and fitness facilities. Parks not only beautify the city but also provide citizens with places to relax and exercise.

From my perspective, the increase in the number of parks reflects the government's commitment to improving people's well-being and promoting sustainable development. This is a positive trend that benefits both the environment and the quality of life of citizens. It is hoped that more efforts will be made in this direction in the future.

### 2023年 - 大学生兼职比例

**题目：** 柱状图显示不同年级大学生兼职比例，高年级学生兼职比例更高。

**参考范文：**

The above bar chart illustrates the proportion of college students who get engaged in part-time jobs before graduation. According to the chart, the percentage of senior students working part-time is 88.24%, which is the highest for all the students. The figure is followed by junior students and sophomores, reaching 71.93% and 71.13% respectively. By contrast, the lowest percentage of part-timer is the freshmen, with only 67.77% involved.

Why do students of higher grade prefer to be part-time workers while fewer freshmen take the kind of jobs? Many senior students choose this job, arguing that they can accumulate working experience by working as an intern in some companies such as banks, advertising agencies and trading firms. In other words, part-time jobs have become incubators for students to develop their social abilities — a boon for their future career. However, as for the freshmen, they are not ready to step into the society partly because they are still too young to recognize the significance of social experience.

From my perspective, part-time jobs provide students with more opportunities to hone their key skills such as communication and cooperation, which will be crucial for students to work their way up the career ladder. Therefore, students should make use of the opportunity to develop their skills needed for their future work.

### 2022年 - 网络购物

**题目：** 折线图或柱状图显示近年网络购物用户数量及交易额的变化趋势。

**参考范文：**

The chart above clearly demonstrates the rapid growth of online shopping in China over the past few years. According to the data, the number of online shoppers has increased dramatically, and the total transaction value has also witnessed a substantial rise.

Several reasons can account for this trend. To begin with, the widespread use of smartphones and the Internet has made online shopping more accessible than ever before. Moreover, e-commerce platforms offer a wider variety of products at competitive prices, which greatly appeals to consumers. Additionally, the convenience of home delivery and easy return policies have further promoted the popularity of online shopping.

In my opinion, while online shopping brings great convenience, consumers should also be cautious about potential risks such as fraud and privacy leakage. It is advisable for both the government and e-commerce platforms to strengthen regulation and protect consumers' rights.

### 2020年 - 手机阅读

**题目：** 图表显示手机阅读用户数量和使用频率的变化趋势。

**参考范文：**

As is clearly shown in the chart, the number of people reading on mobile phones has been on a steady rise in recent years. The data indicates that mobile reading has become an increasingly popular way of accessing information and entertainment.

The reasons for this trend are manifold. First, the rapid development of mobile technology has made smartphones more powerful and affordable. Second, various reading apps and online platforms provide users with abundant reading materials, ranging from news articles to e-books. Third, the fast-paced lifestyle of modern society makes mobile reading a convenient choice for people to make use of their fragmented time.

In my view, mobile reading is a double-edged sword. On the one hand, it enhances the accessibility of knowledge and information. On the other hand, excessive screen time may harm people's eyesight and reduce their ability to focus. Therefore, a balance should be struck between digital reading and traditional reading.

### 2018年 - 企业服务质量

**题目：** 饼图显示消费者选择某餐厅时考虑的因素（服务、价格、环境、口味等）。

**参考范文：**

The pie chart above clearly reveals the various factors that consumers take into consideration when choosing a restaurant. As can be seen, service quality accounts for the largest proportion, taking up 36%, followed by price at 26%. Environment and taste rank third and fourth, with 20% and 18% respectively.

It is evident that service quality has become the most crucial factor influencing consumers' dining choices. This phenomenon reflects the fact that as living standards continue to improve, people are paying more attention to the overall dining experience rather than just the food itself. Good service not only makes customers feel valued but also encourages repeat visits and word-of-mouth promotion.

From my perspective, restaurant owners should attach great importance to improving service quality. Meanwhile, reasonable pricing and comfortable environment are also indispensable elements for business success.

### 2017年 - 博物馆数量与参观人数

**题目：** 线性图显示2013-2015年博物馆数量和参观人数逐年增加。

**参考范文：**

The statistics about museums and their visitors are presented in the line chart. According to the chart, the number of museums in China increased steadily from about 7,000 in 2013 to nearly 8,000 in 2015. Meanwhile, the number of visitors also rose from around 600 million to over 800 million during the same period.

There are several reasons behind this trend. First, with the improvement of living standards, people's cultural needs have increased substantially. Museums, as important cultural institutions, offer people opportunities to learn about history, art and science. Second, the government has been promoting the development of cultural industries and encouraging more people to visit museums. Many museums now offer free admission or discounted tickets, which attracts a larger audience.

In my opinion, the growing popularity of museums is a positive sign of social progress. It shows that people are not only concerned about material wealth but also spiritual enrichment. It is hoped that this trend will continue in the future.

### 2016年 - 大学生旅游目的

**题目：** 柱状图显示大学生选择旅游的主要目的（欣赏风景、缓解压力、交朋友等）。

**参考范文：**

As is illustrated in the bar chart above, college students choose to travel for various purposes. According to the data, "enjoying natural scenery" ranks first with 38%, followed by "relieving pressure" at 28%. "Making friends" and "other reasons" account for 20% and 14% respectively.

It is not difficult to find out the reasons behind this phenomenon. On the one hand, college students today face great pressure from academic studies and job hunting. Traveling provides them with an opportunity to relax and escape from the stressful campus life temporarily. On the other hand, with the improvement of transportation and the prosperity of tourism industry, traveling has become more accessible and affordable for college students.

From my perspective, traveling is indeed beneficial for college students. It not only broadens their horizons but also helps them develop independence and interpersonal skills. Therefore, students should be encouraged to travel during vacations.

### 2014年 - 城乡人口变化

**题目：** 柱状图显示1990、2000、2010年城镇人口和农村人口的变化对比。

**参考范文：**

What is clearly presented in the above chart is the different numbers of urban residents and rural population among 1990, 2000 and 2010. The number of urban residents climbed steadily from 300 million in 1990 to 660 million or so in 2010, while rural population decreased slightly from about 820 million to 680 million or so in the same period.

It is not difficult to come up with some possible factors accounting for this trend. On the one hand, with the rapid development of economy and society, people in mounting numbers crowd into cities in search of employment, a decent living and the excitement of urban life. On the other hand, the economy in the rural districts is comparatively less developed and thus the opportunity to make money and pursue their future is also lean. As a result, an increasing number of people are trying to escape this destiny by flocking into the urban areas.

Given the analyses above, I firmly believe that such established trend will surely continue for quite a while in the forthcoming years and due importance should be attached to this evident situation.

### 1. 申请信（2006年英语一）

**题目：** 你想通过希望工程资助偏远地区儿童，写信给相关部门请求帮助寻找候选人。

**参考范文：**

To Whom It May Concern,

I am writing this letter to ask if there is a child in need of financial sponsorship through the Hope Foundation. I am willing to give financial aid to those children who have not had a chance to receive education in poverty-stricken areas.

I would like to help two kinds of children: first, those who do not have a stable source which can cover their education fees; and second, those whose academic performance in school is excellent. My plan is to cover all the tuition fees and provide necessary learning materials.

There is no doubt that I meet the criteria required for a sponsor and look forward to hearing from you. Thank you for your consideration.

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming

# 第三部分：常用句式与模板

## 一、开头句式

### 1. 图画/图表描述开头

1. As is vividly depicted/illustrated in the picture/cartoon/drawing, ...
   （正如图画生动描绘的那样，...）

2. The picture/cartoon above vividly shows/depicts/illustrates that ...
   （以上图画生动地展示了...）

3. As is symbolically and explicitly demonstrated in the cartoon above, ...
   （正如上图中象征性和明确地展示的那样，...）

4. What the picture above illustrates in a straightforward way is that ...
   （上图直截了当地说明的是...）

5. The cartoon provides us with a thought-provoking scene: ...
   （这幅漫画给我们提供了一个发人深省的情景：...）

6. Portrayed distinctively by the two cartoons above is an impressive scene: ...
   （上面两幅漫画独特描绘的是一个令人印象深刻的场景：...）

7. What is clearly presented in the above chart/graph/table is that ...
   （以上图表清楚地呈现的是...）

8. According to the figures shown in the table/graph, we can see that ...
   （根据表格/图表中显示的数据，我们可以看到...）

9. As is shown in the chart/graph/diagram released by the government, ...
   （正如政府发布的图表所示，...）

10. The chart/graph/table shows a sharp increase/decline in ... compared with those of last year.
    （图表显示，与去年相比，...出现了急剧增长/下降。）

### 2. 现象/话题引入开头

11. With the rapidly growing popularity of ..., the quality of our lives has been considerably changed.
    （随着...的迅速普及，我们的生活质量已经发生了显著变化。）

12. Recently, the problem of ... has been brought to popular attention / has become the focus of public concern.
    （最近，...问题已经引起了广泛关注/成为公众关注的焦点。）

13. With the development of ..., more and more people come to realize that ...
    （随着...的发展，越来越多的人开始意识到...）

14. Now it is commonly/generally/widely believed/held/acknowledged that ...
    （现在人们普遍认为...）

15. There is a growing awareness of the necessity to ...
    （人们越来越意识到...的必要性。）

16. No one would deny that / Everyone would agree that / There is no doubt that ...
    （没有人会否认/每个人都会同意/毫无疑问...）

17. Nowadays, a heated debate/discussion about ... is under way in China.
    （如今，关于...的激烈讨论正在中国展开。）

18. When it comes to ..., most people contend that ..., but other people consider that ...
    （当谈到...时，大多数人主张...，但其他人认为...）

19. It goes without saying that ...
    （不言而喻，...）

20. As the proverb says, "..."
    （正如谚语所说，"..."）

### 3. 书信/应用文开头

21. I am writing this letter to express my interest in ...
    （我写这封信是为了表达我对...的兴趣。）

22. I am writing to extend my heartfelt gratitude/thanks to you for ...
    （我写信是为了衷心地感谢您...）

23. I am writing to express my apology to you for ...
    （我写信是为了因...向您道歉。）

24. I am writing to express my dissatisfaction/complaint about ...
    （我写信是为了表达我对...的不满/投诉。）

25. I am writing to invite you to ...
    （我写信是为了邀请您...）

26. I am writing to make some suggestions / recommendations concerning ...
    （我写信是为了就...提出一些建议。）

27. I am writing to apply for the position of ...
    （我写信是为了申请...的职位。）

28. On behalf of ..., I am writing to extend our warm welcome to you.
    （我代表...写信向您表示热烈欢迎。）

29. Congratulations! I'm so glad to hear that ...
    （祝贺您！我很高兴听说...）

30. I am sorry / I feel so sorry to hear / learn that ...
    （听说...我很难过/很抱歉。）

## 二、过渡句式

### 1. 承上启下

31. There are several reasons accounting for this phenomenon.
    （有几个原因可以解释这一现象。）

32. It is not difficult to come up with some possible factors accounting for this trend.
    （不难找出一些可能的因素来解释这一趋势。）

33. A number of factors could account for / contribute to this change.
    （许多因素可以解释/促成了这一变化。）

34. The reasons for this trend are manifold.
    （这一趋势的原因是多方面的。）

35. This phenomenon can be attributed to several factors.
    （这一现象可归因于几个因素。）

### 2. 列举/递进

36. First and foremost / To begin with / In the first place, ...
    （首先，...）

37. Moreover / Furthermore / In addition / Additionally / Besides, ...
    （此外/而且，...）

38. Last but not least, ...
    （最后但同样重要的是，...）

39. For one thing, ... For another thing, ...
    （一方面，...另一方面，...）

40. On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...
    （一方面，...另一方面，...）

### 3. 对比/转折

41. However / Nevertheless / On the contrary / In contrast, ...
    （然而/相反，...）

42. Although many people hold the view that ..., I still believe that ...
    （虽然许多人持有...的观点，我仍然认为...）

43. It is true that ..., but it does not mean that ...
    （的确...，但这并不意味着...）

44. While it is commonly believed that ..., the truth is that ...
    （虽然人们普遍认为...，但实际上...）

45. Superficially / At first glance, it seems that ... But in fact / on close examination, ...
    （表面上看/乍一看，...似乎...但事实上/仔细审视，...）

### 4. 举例/补充

46. Take ... for example / instance.
    （以...为例。）

47. A case in point is ...
    （一个典型的例子是...）

48. There are numerous examples to illustrate this point.
    （有无数的例子可以说明这一点。）

49. What's more / What's far more important is that ...
    （更重要的是...）

50. It is worth noting that ...
    （值得注意的是...）

### 5. 原因分析

51. This is mainly due to the fact that ...
    （这主要是因为...）

52. The reason lies in the fact that ...
    （原因在于...）

53. ... contributes to / leads to / results in / gives rise to ...
    （...导致了/引起了...）

54. The change in ... mainly stems from / derives from ...
    （...的变化主要源于...）

## 三、结尾句式

55. From my perspective / From my point of view / In my opinion, ...
    （从我的角度来看/在我看来，...）

56. As far as I am concerned, ...
    （就我而言，...）

57. Given the analyses above, I firmly believe that ...
    （鉴于以上分析，我坚信...）

58. From what has been mentioned above, we can come to the conclusion that ...
    （综上所述，我们可以得出结论...）

59. In conclusion / To sum up / In a word / In summary, ...
    （总之/总而言之，...）

60. It is hoped that we should place much emphasis on / pay more attention to ...
    （希望我们应当更加重视...）

61. Only in this way / Only when / Only through ... can we ...
    （只有这样/只有当/只有通过...我们才能...）

62. It is high time that we took effective measures to ...
    （是我们采取有效措施...的时候了。）

63. Therefore, we should not only ... but also ... as well.
    （因此，我们不仅应该...，而且...也。）

64. All the above evidence goes to show that ...
    （以上所有证据表明...）

65. We then have reasons to be confident that in the near future ...
    （我们有理由相信在不久的将来...）

66. My suggestion is that ...; otherwise ...
    （我的建议是...；否则...）

67. So I believe a ... tomorrow will be achieved through efforts of every person.
    （所以我相信通过每个人的努力，一个...的明天将会实现。）

68. It is vital / essential / crucial / imperative for us to ...
    （对我们来说...是至关重要的。）

69. Therefore, we should do our best in eliminating ...
    （因此，我们应该尽力消除...）

70. Who is to say that ...?
    （谁能说...呢？）

## 四、描述图画专用句式

71. The caption reads / says: "..."
    （图下文字写道："..."）

    （画家的目的是表明...）

73. It is most likely that by drawing the picture, the artist intends to ...
    （画家很可能想通过这幅画...）

74. The picture is intended to draw our attention to ...
    （这幅画旨在引起我们对...的关注。）

75. In the first picture, ... While in the second one, ...
    （在第一幅图中，...而在第二幅中，...）

## 五、图表描述专用句式

76. The number of ... grew / rose / increased / climbed from ... to ...
    （...的数量从...增长到...）

77. There was a marked / dramatic / sharp / steady increase / rise / decline / drop in ...
    （...出现了显著/急剧/稳步的增长/下降。）

78. ... accounts for / takes up / represents ... % of the total.
    （...占总数的...%。）

79. The figure is followed by ..., reaching ... %.
    （紧随其后的是...，达到...%。）

80. By contrast / In contrast, the lowest percentage of ... is ...
    （相比之下，...比例最低的是...）

81. ... is twice / three times / half as large / high as ...
    （...是...的两倍/三倍/一半大小/高。）

## 六、小作文常用套句

### 感谢信
82. I am writing to express my sincere gratitude for ...
    （我写信是为了表达我诚挚的感谢...）

83. I would like to thank you from the bottom of my heart for ...
    （我由衷地感谢您...）

84. I appreciate it more than I can say.
    （我对您的感激之情难以言表。）

### 道歉信
85. I sincerely apologize for any inconvenience I may have caused.
    （对于可能造成的任何不便，我深表歉意。）

86. Please accept my sincere apologies for ...
    （请接受我对...的诚挚道歉。）

### 邀请信
87. It is with great pleasure that I invite you to ...
    （我很荣幸邀请您...）

88. We would be greatly honored if you could ...
    （如果您能...我们将不胜荣幸。）

### 建议信
89. I would like to offer some suggestions / recommendations concerning ...
    （我想就...提出一些建议。）

90. I hope you will find these proposals useful / practical.
    （希望您觉得这些建议有用/可行。）

### 投诉信
91. I feel something should be done about ...
    （我觉得应该对...采取措施。）

92. I would appreciate it if you could ... at your earliest convenience.
    （如果您能在方便时尽快...，我将不胜感激。）

### 申请信
93. I am writing in response to your advertisement in ...
    （我写信是为了回应贵方在...上的广告。）

94. I feel I have the necessary qualifications and experience needed for this position.
    （我认为我具备这个职位所需的资格和经验。）

### 通知
95. This notice is to inform you that ...
    （本通知旨在告知您...）

96. All the students are required to ...
    （所有学生都需要...）

## 八、作文结构模板

### 图画作文（英语一）

**第一段：描述图画**
- As is vividly depicted in the picture, ...
- The caption reads: "..."
- We can clearly see that ...

**第二段：阐释寓意+分析原因**
- The purpose of the drawer is to show us that ...
- There are several reasons accounting for this phenomenon.
- First ... Second ... Third ...

**第三段：个人观点+建议**
- From my perspective, ...
- It is high time that we took effective measures to ...
- Only in this way can we ...

### 图表作文（英语二）

**第一段：描述图表**
- As is clearly presented in the chart/graph/table, ...
- The number of ... increased from ... to ...
- ... accounts for ... % of the total.

**第二段：分析原因**
- Several factors can account for this trend.
- On the one hand, ... On the other hand, ...

**第三段：结论/展望**
- Given the analyses above, I firmly believe that ...
- It is hoped that ...

### 小作文（应用文）

**第一段：表明目的**
- I am writing this letter to ...

**第二段：具体内容**
- To begin with / First and foremost, ...
- Moreover / In addition, ...
- Last but not least, ...

**第三段：期待回复/致谢**
- I am looking forward to your early reply.
- Thank you for your time and consideration.
